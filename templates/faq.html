Frequently Asked Questions

What types of math problems can I solve?

You can solve algebra, calculus (differentiation/integration), and basic word problems (area, perimeter, etc.).

How do I use the image upload?

Upload a clear image of your math problem. The app will try to extract and solve it.

Why do some word problems not work?

The app supports common patterns (area, perimeter, etc.). For complex word problems, try to rephrase or use mathematical expressions.

How do I log out?

Click the "Logout" link in the top bar.
